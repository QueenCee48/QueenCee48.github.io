Introduction

My name is Ciera Baucham and I am a Digital Media major at Georgia Gwinnett College. Throughout my college career, I have learned some Python, Java, Processing, HTML, and CSS. I have since become very fond of programming and continue to challenge myself to learn more outside of school. I am also a digital artist and I sometimes do art commissions for clients. One day, I hope to make a career out of my love for art and possibly programming as well.
